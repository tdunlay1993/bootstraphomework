Down Syndrome Redesign

The Down Syndrome Network is a non-profit in Arizona whose mission is too provide info, help, and medical assistance to families with down syndrome. I worked with a team of 3 Ux designers to Re-Vamp this website.

My Role

Ux Designer

Tools

Adobe XD, Miro, Figma, Trello.

Skills

Wireframing, Testing, Prototyping, Interviewing

Timeline

2 Weeks

Why Redesign?

As a non-profit the down syndrome network relies on funding and donations to keep it going. The problem is, as soon as you land on the website it is extremely difficult to navigate the site and find the donation path.
